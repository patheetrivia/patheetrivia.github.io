Sample Work

Below is a taste of the kinds of rounds Pathee creates. Each is designed to balance accessibility and cleverness—so beginners learn something new while seasoned quizzers still get a challenge.

  • Hidden Pronouns Round – Ten answers conceal a 3‑letter pronoun inside longer words or phrases.
  • Before & After Chain – Classic Jeopardy‑style mash‑ups (e.g., “The Godfather of Pearl”).
  • Georgist Grab Bag – Economics trivia framed around land‑value tax history.
  • Sneaky Homophones – Clues where the answer pairs sound alike (e.g., “serial” & “cereal”).
  • Timeline Twist – Arrange five historical events in chronological order—sounds easier than it is!
